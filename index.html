Scam awareness

Earn online without falling for traps.

Z Guide flags common scams beginners face when learning online skills and freelancing.

Fake courses

Avoid courses promising guaranteed income with no portfolio, practice, or client acquisition system.

Fake income claims

Ignore screenshots without context. Real growth includes skill depth, proof, positioning, and consistent outreach.

Scam clients

Use milestones, deposits, written scope, and platform protection before sending final files.

Pyramid schemes

Do not pay to recruit people. Choose skills that create real business value and transferable proof.
